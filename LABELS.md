# Label Structure

## Company Labels
- `ziply` - Ziply Fiber contract work and LFG staffing opportunities
- `fractal-shift` - AI consulting, LegacyLift, strategic initiatives
- `personal` - Personal projects, health, family, life admin

## Priority Labels  
- `p0` - Urgent/critical, needs immediate attention
- `p1` - High priority, this week
- `p2` - Medium priority, this month
- `p3` - Low priority, someday/maybe

## Type Labels
- `strategic` - High-level planning, business strategy, major decisions
- `tactical` - Execution-focused, project work, implementations
- `technical` - Engineering, infrastructure, code, DevOps work
- `admin` - Paperwork, compliance, routine administrative tasks

## Status Labels
- `blocked` - Cannot proceed due to external dependency
- `waiting-on-others` - Waiting for response/action from someone else
- `in-progress` - Actively being worked on
- (No label = Backlog)
- (Closed = Done)

## Colors (for manual label creation if needed)
- Company: Blue tones (#0366d6, #1f77d4, #0e8a8a)  
- Priority: Red to yellow (#d73a4a, #e99695, #fbca04, #c5def5)
- Type: Purple tones (#6f42c1, #7057ff, #a371f7, #d4c5f9)
- Status: Orange/yellow (#d93f0b, #fbca04)
